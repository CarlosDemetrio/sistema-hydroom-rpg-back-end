# T10.2 - OWASP Error Leak Prevention

## Objective
Ensure error responses do not leak stack traces or internal identifiers.

## Steps
1. Review `GlobalExceptionHandler` responses.
2. Remove stack traces from responses.
3. Verify status codes and messages remain clear.

## Acceptance Checks
- No stack traces in responses.
- No internal IDs leaked.
